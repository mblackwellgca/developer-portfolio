About

Welcome to my portofolio! My name is Michelle Blackwell and I am glad you stopped by to check out my work. I have been an educator since 2007 teaching math and history while serving as tech support for my school from 2008-2018. In 2018 I began a new journey in serving my school more formally on the tech side as the Systems and Programs Specialist and now Coordinator. Learning to code and web development is my newest journey and I am excited to grow in this field.


A quick study with an efficient mindset, my goal is to create and streamline web-based applications with my schools current programs. It amazes me that current programs in education are so open-source and allow users to create what they need to work more effictively and efficently, if we just knew how to do so! My goal is to learn the ins and outs so that I can assist my staff with learning ways to work smarter, not harder!
